Introduction

In September, Daimler Trucks North America briefed us on their endeavour to create a system of autonomous trucks with a connected platform, Mission Control. This challenge in innovation raised numerous questions in human-machine and human-computer interaction.We have researched, analyzed, and designed a solution we believe to be novel and valuable to the problem space.

Research

We began our research with the intent to understand the current trucking industry today and how autonomy might affect the current industry. Our research was guided by the following questions in mind: How do DTNA’s customers’ businesses operate? Who are the main stakeholders? What are the implications of moving to vehicle autonomy? How will this affect the drivers and other stakeholders?
