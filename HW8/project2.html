Planning Research

Initially, we as a team had some background and desk research on the topic, so we use this to guide the creation of further research quesion to help guide us.

Why do users make reports on social media?

How do users perceive the feedback given to them after they report something on a social media website?

What do users know about the algorithms that shape the products they use?

Conducting a reframing activity that helps us define our project.

Problem Scope Definition

By reframing the larger problem space of ‘everyday algorithm auditing,’ we were able to narrow on a topic that we believed could provide some insight for the larger problem space

How might we incentivize people to participate in reporting algorithmic bias they find on social media platforms?
